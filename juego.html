FAQs

How do you play?

Campus Bonanza is an RPG-style game where you participate in minigames located in different campus areas. Each minigame represents a real incident (such as trash or broken equipment), and you must solve it within a time limit to earn points and improve your skills.

How do you win?

You win by accumulating points as you solve incidents in the minigames. The faster and more efficiently you act, the more points you earn. You can use those points to get upgrades and climb the leaderboard.

How does the score system work?

You earn points for each task or incident solved within the minigames. Your performance is recorded and displayed on a general leaderboard where you can track your progress and compare it with other players.

How do I register on the scoreboard?

Simply create an account with your name, email, and password. Once logged in, your gameplay results will be automatically tracked and added to the scoreboard.

How do power-ups work?

You can use the points you've earned to purchase power-ups in the game's shop. These enhance your performance in the minigames by giving you advantages like extended time, increased speed, or specialized abilities.
