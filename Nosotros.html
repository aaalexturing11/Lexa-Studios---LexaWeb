LEXA Studios is a team of students from Tecnológico de Monterrey focused on developing interactive solutions with social impact. Our main project, Campus Bonanza, is an RPG-style video game designed to improve the university environment by engaging students in the detection and resolution of campus issues. We combine technology, creativity, and social purpose to create innovative experiences that promote well-being, collaboration, and community awareness.

Mission

To develop interactive and educational tools that promote student participation in the improvement of the university environment, encouraging learning, social responsibility, and collaborative work.

Values

Commitment, creativity, innovation, collaboration, social responsibility, and respect for the student community and its environment.

Vision

To become a benchmark in the development of gamified solutions that inspire positive change in educational communities through technology and user-centered design.

Technologies Used

Scope

Campus Bonanza is focused on students at the Monterrey Campus. The system includes user registration, role-based login, minigames, power-up store, leaderboard, and incident reporting system. It runs on modern browsers through WebGL and has growth potential with new mechanics, scenarios, and AI-based improvements.
